The Great War: Redux is a standalone rework of the famous Great War mod from Wolferos. 
[img]https://i.imgur.com/q8cpY5E.png [/img]
Updated focus trees and gameplay for more diverse, playable, historical and interesting game.

Compatible with the game version [b]1.16.* [/b].

[h1]This is an independent mod. You will [b]not[/b] need the original TGW.[/h1]

[h1]Features:[/h1]
[list][*]1910 and 1914 scenarios.
[*]New Focuses:
[table equalcells=1]
[tr][td]Russia[/td][td]Britain[/td][td]France[/td][td]Germany[/td][td]Italy[/td][td]Ottoman Empire[/td][td]Serbia[/td][td]Austria-Hungary[/td][/tr]
[tr][td]Bulgaria[/td][td]Greece[/td][td]Romania[/td][td]China (Qing)[/td][td]Netherlands[/td][td]USA[/td][td]Canada[/td][td]Brazil[/td][/tr]
[tr][td]Sweden[/td][td]Belgium[/td][td]Korea[/td][/tr]
[/table]
[*]New ideas, events, solutions, missions.
[*]New 3D models for units from the First World War and the interwar period (Interbellum).
[*]New branches of technologies, divisions, and equipment.
[*]Major map changes.
[*]Unique mechanics for different countries.[/cunning]
